## Notable Components

### Conch Street (Core Residential Block)

- **Structure:** Three residences in a row — pineapple, Easter Island head, rock
- **Analog in cyberspace:** Closest to co-located kingdoms on the same substrate — different architectures, shared geography, constant interaction
- **Political dynamic:** SpongeBob's enthusiasm, Squidward's resentment, Patrick's obliviousness — three governance styles in permanent proximity
- **The rock has no door.** Patrick lifts it. This is not a metaphor. The rock literally has no door.

### The Krusty Krab vs. The Chum Bucket

- **Separated by:** One road
- **Economic disparity:** One has all the customers. The other has a computer wife and zero revenue.
- **The Krabby Patty Secret Formula:** The most contested intellectual property in the substrate. Has survived thousands of theft attempts. Has never been disclosed. The formula is the substrate's most protected state secret.

### Sandy's Treedome

- **Unique in the substrate:** The only air-sealed environment in an aquatic dimension
- **Analog in physical dimension:** Like an embassy — sovereign territory of a foreign dimension (Texas / the surface) operating within Bikini Bottom's jurisdiction
- **Requires:** Visitors must wear water helmets. Sandy wears an air helmet outside. Both sides accommodate. This is the only successful bidimensional immigration in the substrate.

### Rock Bottom

- **Unique in the substrate:** A city that exists below Bikini Bottom — the substrate's substrate
- **Language barrier:** Residents speak English but insert a raspberry sound between every word. Communication is technically possible but practically exhausting.
- **Bus service:** One bus. Unreliable. The connection between the two cities is as tenuous as the schedule.
- **The map does not judge Rock Bottom.** It documents it.
